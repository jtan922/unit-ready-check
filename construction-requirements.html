Acceptance Requirements

Maint-20210326

Use these guidelines to inspect units before accepting from construction. List any defects or items that need to be corrected on the Unit Inspection Form for construction supervisor follow-up.

Front Door/Door Frame

  • Door and frame have no scratches, dings, or dents; paint is uniform.
  • Unit address is level and in perfect condition.
  • Door opens and closes easily with no squeaking.
  • Deadbolt, management keys, knocker, peephole, sweep, weather-stripping, threshold, and exterior light are installed and working.
  • Door is aligned with no gaps between door, frame, bottom, or threshold.
  • Threshold is clean, at proper height, and free from dents.

Painted Surfaces Through-Out

  • Paint is uniform on walls, trim, and ceilings, with no wrong-sheen touch-ups.
  • Surfaces are free of scratches, dings, marks, scuffs, dirt, nail pops, dabs, drips, and overspray.
  • Cut-ins are crisp; wall paint is not on ceilings and ceiling paint is not on walls.
  • Walls are smooth with no bows, waviness, visible seams, visible drywall tape, or poor corners.
  • Trim is tight, secure, caulked, painted, and free of exposed nail heads.

Windows Through-Out

  • Windows open, close, hold position, and lock smoothly.
  • Glass, seals, cracks, screens, screen frames, caulking, and channels are clean and in good condition.
  • Windows are clean inside and outside with no stickers or glue residue.
  • Blinds are secure and easy to operate, with no missing wands, bent slats, or frayed strings.
  • Warning labels are removed and cords are untied.
  • Check for drafts, sill gaps, frame caulking to drywall, and signs of water leaking.

Electrical Outlets Through-Out

  • Test all outlets with an outlet meter.
  • Put a small red dot on outlets tied to a wall light switch.
  • Test all GFI outlets.
  • Note any GFI breakers controlling outlets in different rooms.

Flooring (Carpet & Non-Carpet)

  • Flooring is clean, uniform in color, undamaged, and stain-free.
  • Seams and transition strips are neat, tight, and undetectable where possible.
  • No bumps, lumps, holes, failed underlayment, concrete defects, glue showing, or paint spills.
  • Floor material reaches walls with no gaps; scratches are filled or colored in.
  • Toe kicks are properly installed under base cabinets.
  • Proper sealcoat is applied where required.

Living Room/Dining Room

  • Apply painted surfaces, flooring, electrical, and window standards.
  • Patio/balcony door opens, closes, latches, locks, aligns, and seals properly.
  • Glass and tracks are clean and free of construction debris.
  • Patio screen door is clean, sticker-free, on track, smooth, and lockable.
  • Vertical blinds are properly mounted and cut no more than 1/4 inch above the floor.

Patio/Balcony

  • Deck and siding are clean and free of debris.
  • Railings are properly installed and no nails protrude from decking.
  • Trim around patio door has no gaps.
  • Flashing is properly installed where deck attaches to building.
  • Outside light and outdoor GFCI are operational.

Kitchen

  • Apply painted surfaces, flooring, electrical, and window standards.
  • Cabinets have no major scratches, nicks, chunks missing, dust, loose hardware, warped doors, missing stops, or shelf clip issues.
  • Cabinet doors hang straight, close properly, and drawers are clean and smooth.
  • Microwave, exhaust fan, stove, oven, dishwasher, disposal, refrigerator, and icemaker are clean, undamaged, level, secure, and operational.
  • Check burners, oven elements, turntable, microwave light, exhaust fan filter, dishwasher fill, racks, brackets, disposal noise, and disposal wrench.
  • Check faucet, sprayer, hot/cold connection, sink, drain stoppers, caulking, and all under-cabinet plumbing for leaks.
  • Refrigerator supply line is secure, leak-free, not kinked, and icemaker is left off after testing.
  • Granite is smooth, sealed, caulked neatly, and chips/nicks are filled and colored properly.
  • Pantry doors, shelves, rods, appliance manuals, kitchen floor under appliances, lights, rope lighting, dimmers, and fixtures are complete and clean.

Bathroom(s)

  • Apply painted surfaces, flooring, electrical, and window standards.
  • Vanity cabinets, drawers, shelves, hardware, and toe kicks are clean, aligned, secure, and free of water stains or leaks.
  • Towel bars, robe hooks, towel rings, tissue holders, shower rods, mirrors, and accessories are clean, level, and secure.
  • Faucets, pop-up drains, sinks, caulking, hot/cold indicators, bath lights, vent fan, toilet, shower, tub, diverter, shower head, drains, doors, and flooring are tested.
  • Toilet flush, flapper, handle, seat, base caulk, supply line, escutcheon, cleanliness, and moisture at base are checked.
  • Shower tile, grout, glass, tracks, chrome, silicone, tub cleanliness, drain holding, and debris-free drain are checked.
  • Bathroom door opens, closes, latches, locks, and bathroom key is stored on top of outside door trim.
  • During final walk, remove showerhead water saver and adjust the anti-scald feature in the shower control.

Bedroom(s)

  • Apply painted surfaces, flooring, electrical, and window standards.
  • Closet door opens, closes, and latches easily.
  • Pull-test closet shelving for secure installation.
  • Closet light operates properly.
  • Label switched outlet.

Washer/Dryer Utility Closet

  • Apply painted surfaces, flooring, electrical, and window standards.
  • Run washer cycle to verify fill, spin, drain, and correct hot/cold indicators.
  • Run dryer and confirm operation and heat.
  • Machines are clean, sticker-free, and dust-free.
  • Flooring, doors, double-door alignment, latches, and shelf security are checked.

Hot Water Heater/HVAC/Furnace Room

  • Apply painted surfaces, flooring, electrical, and window standards.
  • Set hot water heater to 120 degrees.
  • Furnace filter is new and dated.
  • Return/vent cover, furnace room door, equipment tops, water meter, and transponder number are checked.
  • With two people, verify thermostat connects to the correct outside A/C unit.
  • Test furnace heat, A/C cooling, and airflow from registers in all rooms.
  • Set thermostat to 60 degrees in winter or 80 degrees in summer.

Electricity

  • With two people, verify electric meter is connected to the correct apartment by turning power off at the main meter while someone inside confirms the matching unit loses power.
